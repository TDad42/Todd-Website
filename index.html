About Todd

Todd Anderson is a lead trumpet player specializing in musical theater, production shows, and similar stage shows. With experience on Broadway-style tours (including Elf The Musical and Jersey Boys) as well as multiple cruise ship contracts, Todd delivers a consistent, polished sound tailored to each performance. Whether playing classic jazz tunes or contemporary pop hits, Todd takes a reliable, no-nonsense approach on stage—always ready to provide the right blend of power and finesse for any show.
